# Problem Statement & Product Definition

## Introduction
Media consumers today are overwhelmed by the number of shows available across multiple platforms. This leads to "Decision Paralysis" and "Progress Confusion"â€”users forget which episode they are on and lose track of their viewing habits over time.

## End User Profile
* **Target:** Heavy viewers of episodic content (Anime, TV Series).
* **Capabilities:** Comfortable with basic web/mobile apps and manual data entry.
* **Deficiencies:** Struggles to remember watch-history across different services; lacks awareness of their own viewing habits/time spent.

## Quantified Value Proposition
Our tracker eliminates the "What should I watch?" debate by providing a clear backlog and habit metrics. It saves the user approximately **10 minutes per night** of searching and provides immediate awareness of screen-time distribution, helping users manage their leisure time better.

## Product Core: Habit Awareness
The single differentiator of this product is **Personal Viewing Analytics**. While streaming apps only want you to watch *more*, our tool helps you see *what* you watch. By tracking genre distribution and time spent, we give the user a "Spotify Wrapped" style insight into their own entertainment habits.
